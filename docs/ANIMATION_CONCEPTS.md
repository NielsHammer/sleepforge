# SleepForge — Remotion Animation Concepts

20 philosophy-appropriate animations for screen-blend overlay. All render on a pure black background
so the `blend=screen` composite makes black pixels drop away, leaving only the animation visuals.
Each is 3–5 seconds, designed for sleep-tone pacing. No data visualization, no charts, no numbers.

## Implemented (engine/remotion/components/animations/)

| ID | File | Duration | Description |
|----|------|----------|-------------|
| `RipplesAnimation` | RipplesAnimation.jsx | 3s | Stone drops into still water — concentric chalk rings expand and fade |
| `CandleAnimation` | CandleAnimation.jsx | 3s | Candle with flickering flame; wax pool at base |
| `PathsDivergingAnimation` | PathsDivergingAnimation.jsx | 4s | Single chalk path drawn upward, splits at fork into two diverging roads |
| `HandReleasingAnimation` | HandReleasingAnimation.jsx | 3s | Fist opens slowly; bird-shape rises from palm and drifts upward |
| `HourglassAnimation` | HourglassAnimation.jsx | 4s | Sand flows from upper to lower chamber; particles stream through neck |

## Remaining Concepts (not yet implemented)

6. **ChainSnappingAnimation** — A chain of links drawn across the frame; the center link fractures and
   both sides fall away. Symbol of breaking free from obligation or habit.

7. **SmokeDissolveAnimation** — A simple face outline drawn in chalk lines; the lines gradually unravel
   into wisps of smoke that drift upward and vanish. Symbol of impermanence of ego.

8. **LotusBloomingAnimation** — Petals unfurl in sequence from a tight bud, one by one, each revealing
   the next inner layer. Symbol of gradual awakening.

9. **ArrowReleasingAnimation** — A bow drawn taut, arrow nocked; slow release, the arrow arcs across
   the frame and exits. Symbol of commitment and letting the moment fly.

10. **OpenDoorAnimation** — A simple door outline swings open; warm light floods through from the other
    side. Symbol of acceptance and possibility.

11. **FootprintsErasedAnimation** — Chalk footprints trail across sand; a wave slowly erases them from
    right to left. Symbol of impermanence, "the present moment is all."

12. **MirrorCrackingAnimation** — A chalk oval (mirror shape); fracture lines radiate outward from a
    single point. Symbol of the end of self-deception.

13. **FlameMultiplyingAnimation** — One flame divides into two, then four; each smaller. Symbol of
    ideas spreading, or the illusion that we can control influence.

14. **RopeUnravelingAnimation** — A tightly knotted rope drawn in profile; knot loosens strand by
    strand from the center. Symbol of releasing what binds us.

15. **StarFadingInDawnAnimation** — A single bright point dims as the sky around it lightens from
    black through deep blue to pale grey. Symbol of ego dissolving in the greater whole.

16. **RootsGrowingAnimation** — Perspective from underground: a seed sprouts; roots branch downward
    into the dark earth. Symbol of inner growth invisible from the surface.

17. **BalancingScalesAnimation** — Two hanging pans tilt slowly until they find equilibrium and rest
    still. Symbol of equanimity and acceptance.

18. **ButterflyEmergesAnimation** — A chrysalis shape vibrates; crack lines form; a butterfly outline
    unfolds its wings and holds open. Symbol of transformation.

19. **SandMandalaMeltingAnimation** — A complex mandala pattern slowly dissolves from the center
    outward into formless sand. Symbol of Buddhist impermanence.

20. **HandWritingLetterAnimation** — A chalk hand writes a single word ("release", "accept", "let go")
    in slow deliberate strokes, then the word fades. Symbol of conscious intention.
